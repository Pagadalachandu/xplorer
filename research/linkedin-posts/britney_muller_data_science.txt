Subject: Entity Moats and Agentic Commerce
Source: Britney Muller (LinkedIn/AI Educator)
Key Insight: In 2026, traditional rankings are becoming obsolete due to real-time personalization. The new goal is "Attribution"—making sure AI engines are forced to cite your name.
The "Entity Moat" Framework:
​Branded Datasets: Build unique datasets that AI models cannot replicate. If they can't replicate the data, they must cite the source.
​Brand Mentions as Backlinks: LLMs learn from mentions across the web. Focus on getting cited in high-quality digital ecosystems, not just getting a hyperlink.
​Agentic Commerce: AI assistants are now making buying decisions. Optimize your content so it is "readable" by these agents during the customer journey.
​Think Like an Engineer: Marketers must move from "tasks" (writing) to "shipping tools" (automating data analysis).
Takeaway: If an AI can't verify who you are through your unique data, you don't exist in the 2026 search landscape. Build an entity that AI is forced to cite.
