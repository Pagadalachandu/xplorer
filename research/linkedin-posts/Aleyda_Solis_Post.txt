Subject: Technical SEO Automation and GSC Insights
Source: Aleyda Solís (LinkedIn)
Key Insight: Utilizing specialized AI-integrated suites like "GSC Wizard" to enhance Google Search Console data analysis.
Tool Features:
​Enhanced Dashboard: Tracks Queries, Pages, Rank Changes, and Topic Clusters.
​Optimization: Identifies content decay, cannibalization, and CTR benchmarks.
​Technical Insights: Opportunity scoring, forecasting, and indexing tracking.
Takeaway: The future of Technical SEO relies on building custom dashboards on top of raw GSC data to identify "low-hanging fruit" opportunities and fix page poaching issues automatically.
