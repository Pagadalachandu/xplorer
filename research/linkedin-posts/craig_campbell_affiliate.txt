Subject: Diversifying Traffic and Personal Branding in 2026
Source: Craig Campbell (LinkedIn/SEO Specialist)
Key Insight: SEO is no longer a "one-source" game. If you rely 100% on Google, your business is at risk. 2026 is about building an "Ecosystem" around your brand.
The "Anti-Fragile" SEO Strategy:  
​Diversify Traffic: Shift focus toward YouTube, Spotify, and TikTok. These are the new "search engines" where AI agents look for human signals.
​Foreign SEO: Look for opportunities in non-English markets (like the Netherlands or India) where AI competition is lower but the "cost of retrieval" is still profitable.
​Selling Links & Authority: Backlinks still matter in 2026, but only if they come from real, traffic-generating ecosystems rather than dead "link farms."
​Personal Brand Moat: Video content is the hardest thing for AI to fake convincingly. Building a face-to-camera brand is the ultimate protection against AI content saturation.
Takeaway: Stop trying to "beat" the algorithm and start building an audience that searches for your name specifically.
