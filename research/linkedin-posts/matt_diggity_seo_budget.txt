Subject: SEO Budgeting and Organic Growth Strategy
​Source: Matt Diggity (LinkedIn)
​Key Insight: You do not necessarily need a massive budget to move the needle in SEO.
​Data Point: Observed organic search growth from March 2024 to September 2025 showing a significant percentage increase in engaged sessions.
​Takeaway: Focus on high-impact, low-cost "personality frameworks" and efficient data-driven tools rather than just high spending.
