Subject: Multi-Engine Domination Framework
Source: Julian Goldie (LinkedIn/Social)

The "4-Step Content Machine":
​Claude for high-quality scripts.
​HeyGen/AI Avatars for video production.
​ElevenLabs for voice cloning.
​Distribution: Repurposing one core video into 50+ pieces of content across Reddit, YouTube, and Newsletters.
Takeaway: Speed of execution is the new moat. Use AI to build a "brand presence" everywhere so that LLMs learn to cite you as the primary authority.
