Subject: The "SEO Product Fit" in the AI Era
Source: Kevin Indig (LinkedIn)
Key Insight: AI changes the "Growth Loop." SEO is no longer just about keywords; it’s about how well your product answers the user's ultimate goal.
Data Point: Rise in "Answer Engine Optimization" (AEO) where AI bots summarize content directly.
Takeaway: Brands must focus on unique, proprietary data that AI cannot easily scrape or replicate to stay competitive in search results.
