Subject: E-E-A-T and the 2026 AI Crackdown
Source: Lily Ray (LinkedIn/Amsive)
Key Insight: Google is moving toward "Entity-Based" search. It's no longer enough to have keywords; the AI must recognize the author as a real person with verified credentials.
Core Principles for the Playbook:
​Digital PR: Focus on earning mentions on high-authority sites (.gov, .edu, and major news outlets) to prove authority.
​Authenticity: Use original photos, first-person case studies, and unique research that AI cannot hallucinate.
​The "vicious cycle": Avoid "scaling" AI content too quickly, as Google's 2026 updates are designed to sitewide-demote sites that prioritize quantity over human-verified quality.
Takeaway: Trust is the only moat left. If an AI search engine (like Perplexity or ChatGPT) can't verify who you are through third-party mentions, it won't cite you.
