Subject: The Koray Framework and Topical Authority
Source: Koray Tuğberk GÜBÜR (LinkedIn/Holistic SEO)
Key Insight: Rank Topics, Not Keywords. To survive AI search, a site must cover every semantic variation of a niche (Topical Coverage) to build a history of reliability (Historical Data).
Core Formulas for the Playbook:
​Topical Authority = Topical Coverage + Historical Data. You must map out all sub-topics before writing a single word.
​Cost of Retrieval: Structure your content so it is "machine-first." This means using clear semantic hierarchies and entity connections that an AI can parse in milliseconds.
​Algorithmic Authorship: Use specific word sequences and factual certainty. Avoid "fluff" or opinion-based filler that complicates the AI's understanding.
Takeaway: If your site has a "low cost of retrieval," it has a much higher chance of being the primary source cited in Google AI Overviews and Perplexity.
